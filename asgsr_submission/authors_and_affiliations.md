## Notes
- Confirm each contributor consents to authorship and to publication in the
  ASGSR proceedings.
- If the AstroBotany lab's published spaceflight work is used only as background
  (not as the team's own result), keep those investigators in the
  **Acknowledgments/References**, not the author list, unless they actively
  contributed to *this* submission.
- ORCIDs are optional but recommended; add them to `CITATION.cff` too.
