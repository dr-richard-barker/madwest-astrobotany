# Figure legends

> Note: ASGSR **abstracts are text-only** (no figures). These figures accompany
> the full **manuscript** / poster, not the abstract submission.

**Figure 1. TREES (*Populus tremula* × ectomycorrhiza): mycorrhizal seedlings die
back.** Mean per-plant **start → peak → end** green-canopy area by treatment.
The +ECM (mycorrhizal) groups end well below their peak (0.71 flown, 0.73 ground;
labels show end as % of peak), whereas −ECM groups hold near peak (0.96, 0.94) —
the fungal symbiosis did not aid flight survival. *Caveat: +ECM groups were
imaged ~9 days vs. ~6 for −ECM, confounding the comparison.*
*(file: `fig1_canopy_dieback.png`; growth curves `fig1b_canopy_curves.png`;
per-plant-normalized trajectories `fig1c_relative_growth.png`.)*

**Figure 2. qPCR stress-gene response to flight (ΔΔCt).** log2 fold-change of
MPK3, TCH3 and CBP60g (normalized to UBQ10) in Flight and Launch-site groups
relative to the lab Ground-control calibrator, from the *Arabidopsis* launch.
Error bars span the biological-replicate range (n = 4). Flight trends: MPK3 ↓
(0.34×), TCH3 ↓ (0.56×), CBP60g ↑ (1.60×) — trends, not significance.
*(file: `fig2_qpcr_foldchange.png`.)*

**Figure 3. Root gravitropism under spectral light.** (a) Root-tip angle over
time under blue, green, purple, red and white light ("RootTrace"); (b) root
length by light treatment ("RootNav"). Slides from the gravitropism assay deck.
*(files: `fig3a_roottrace_angle.jpg`, `fig3b_rootnav_length.jpg`.)*
